Additional Needs

Every student’s needs are different. We offer adjustments, assistive technology, and one-to-one support so you can participate fully in university life.

What We Offer

  • One-to-one study skills support
  • Assistive technology and training
  • Exam and coursework adjustments
  • Accessible learning materials

Assistive Technology

Screen readers, text-to-speech, mind-mapping tools, and training to use them confidently.

Learn more

Exam Adjustments

Extra time, rest breaks, smaller/quiet rooms, and use of a computer or AT where needed.

Learn more

Accessible Campus

Step-free routes, lifts, accessible toilets, quiet/sensory spaces, and prayer/faith rooms.

Learn more

Study Support

Note-taking support, mentoring, study-skills coaching, and accessible course materials.

Learn more
Did you know? If you have a disability or learning difference, you may be eligible for the Disabled Students’ Allowance (DSA) to help with study-related costs.
